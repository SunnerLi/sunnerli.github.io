I work as a researcher near TCC Group Holdings in Taoyuan. My goal is to advance AI models (particularly generative models) or systems to enhance their capabilities, enabling applications across diverse modalities and domain. I am currently focused on developing systems by leveraging foundation models, prompt engineering and retrieval augmented generation.

I initially worked as a Computer Vision and Deep Learning Research Engineer, and later as an AI Engineer at MediaTek. I obtained my Master’s degree in Institute of Computer Science and Engineering from National Chiao Tung University, where I was advised by Prof. Yu-Tai Ching and worked with Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu and Dr. Li Su. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Taipei University, where I collaborated with Prof. Bor-Shing Lin.

Outside of work, I am also looking for my life partner and hope to be your sunshine ☀️. Please feel free to contact me if you’re interested.
